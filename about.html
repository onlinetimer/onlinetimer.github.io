Perfect timer for students & lazy people! or both! :))
Online Timer visualy shows, how much of your day was wasted!

We are currently in quarantine and everyone, including me, are studying and following lecutes from home. But after two weeks, I still didn't manage to organize my time. All I did was programming and other fun stuff.

After trying many different online timers, none were quite what I wanted. So I decident to make my own timer with all the features that I needed. And most importantly, it has a giant graph that makes me more aware of the passing time.

Tutorial (✿◠‿◠)

Full circle represents 24 hours so that means 1 hour is about 15°.
Midnight ~ 0° and 360° Midday ~ 180°. When you click START diagram will now start a green trace (arc) indicating time spent working/studying.

At the end of the day, you are left with a "cake diagram" of you activity.
